Advanced Radiation Shielding Solutions (Lead Shielding)

Astra Shield Electromechanical Works LLC provides custom-engineered, high-end radiation shielding systems for industrial, nuclear, and medical applications. Our solutions are designed to protect personnel, equipment, and environments from various types of ionizing radiation, including: X-Rays (lead Shielding)
Gamma Rays

Astra Shield's radiation shielding capabilities encompass:

  • Lead Shielding Systems: Utilizing high-density lead panels, bricks, doors, and partitions to absorb high-energy gamma and X-ray radiation.

  • Concrete Shielding Structures:Constructing poured or precast heavy concrete walls and vaults for high-intensity installations like nuclear reactors or linear accelerators (LINACs).

  • Specialized Composite Shielding: Employing advanced materials such as tungsten and borated polyethylene to achieve stringent shielding performance in confined spaces.

  • Shielded Doors & Windows: Providing radiation-proof sliding or hinged doors with integrated lead cores, as well as shielded view panels for controlled access zones.

  • Site-Specific Design & Installation: Developing tailored shielding layouts based on thorough radiation hazard assessments, dose modelling, and adherence to regulatory standards.

Astra Shield adheres to a range of compliance standards, including:

  • IAEA Radiation Protection Guidelines
  • NCRP Reports (National Council on Radiation Protection)
  • IEC 61331 and IEC 60601
  • Local UAE & GCC Radiation Safety Regulations
  • FANAR Regulations of UAE
