PROJECT MANAGEMENT

At Astra Shield, we offer comprehensive turnkey solutions for medical imaging facilities and Defence/Industrial shielding environments. Our approach ensures that every project is managed from the ground up — starting with site evaluation, followed by customized design, precise execution, and finally, comprehensive testing and commissioning.
We take ownership of the entire process, so our clients can rely on a single, specialized partner from concept to completion.

MEDICAL PROJECTS – IMAGING & RADIATION SHIELDING

Astra Shield provides end-to-end turnkey solutions for hospitals, diagnostic centers, and radiology departments. The scope of our medical projects includes:

  • Initial Site Study & Evaluation:This involves understanding the room layout, existing infrastructure, OEM equipment specifications, and client needs.

  • Engineering Design & Planning:Services encompass CAD-based layouts, detailed shielding analysis, interior design concept planning, and coordination of various services.

  • Shielding Works: We implement various shielding solutions:

  • Machine Supply & Installation: Astra Shield take care of installation of major diagnostic imaging equipment for Siemens, Philips, GE, Fujifilm, Canon etc. This covers professional setup and integration of imaging systems including MRI, CT, Mammography, Fluoroscopy, and X-ray.

  • Mechanical & Electrical Services: Our offerings include HVAC ducting and ventilation, electrical conduiting, cabling, and grounding, trenching for services, and ceiling structure design, simulation, and installation.

  • Interior Works:Complete interior ID finishing, such as painting, flooring, anti-bacterial finishes, mood lights, picture panels, sky ceilings, and MRI-compatible furnishings.

  • Testing & Commissioning: This involves RF attenuation testing, EMI and magnetic shielding verification, and final project handover with comprehensive compliance documentation.

Shielding Works: We implement various shielding solutions:

  • RF shielding enclosures for MRI.
  • Lead shielding for X-ray, CT, fluoroscopy, PET-CT, and mammography rooms.
  • Acoustic shielding for noise reduction and patient comfort for MRI Facilities.
  • RF Shielded Doors
  • RF Shielded Window

DEFENSE & INDUSTRIAL PROJECTS – SHIELDING & HARDENING

Astra Shield also delivers specialized turnkey solutions for mission-critical and high-security environments, including military, research, and utility infrastructure. The scope of our defence and industrial projects includes:

  • Site Assessment & Requirement Analysis: Understanding threat levels, compliance needs (e.g., SCIF, HEMP), and functional objectives.

  • Shielding System Design & Engineering::This involves customized shielding designs adhering to MIL, NSA, and IEC standards, along with waveguide, EMP filter, and grounding configurations.

  • Interior & Environmental Setup: This includes climate control, fire resistance, and secure interior finishes where applicable.

  • Testing, Certification & Final Handover: Services cover EMI testing, magnetic field measurements, and provision of compliance reports and certification.

Supply & Installation: We provide and install:

  • SCIF shielding.
  • EMP/HEMP shielding.
  • Magnetic shielding.
  • Neutron shielding.
