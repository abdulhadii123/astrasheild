Diagnostic Radiology Site Preparation

At Astra Shield, we provide comprehensive site preparation for diagnostic radiology facilities, delivering turnkey infrastructure solutions tailored to each project’s clinical, technical, and safety requirements. From initial design through to final finishing, we ensure your imaging suite is fully equipped and optimized for performance and patient comfort.

Our Radiology Site Preparation Services Include:

  • Room Design & Layout Planning:Optimized for patient workflow, equipment placement, shielding zones, and technician accessibility.

  • Ceiling Structures: Design, simulation, fabrication, and installation of robust ceiling-mounted supports for CT, X-ray, and fluoroscopy equipment, engineered to meet OEM load and safety requirements.

  • Electrical & Cabling Systems: Complete electrical setup including power distribution, UPS backup, grounding, along with professional cabling and conduiting to support imaging systems and IT infrastructure.

  • HVAC & Ducting Installation: Specialized HVAC design with precise temperature and humidity control, including duct installation to manage airflow, ventilation, and MRI cryogen venting needs.

  • Trenching & Mechanical Works: Installation of trenches for cabling and piping, plus mechanical supports for heavy equipment and accessories.

  • Interior Finishing & Design: Comprehensive interior finishing services including painting, interior décor (ID) works, installation of mood lighting, RGB lighting systems, picture panels, and MRI-compatible materials to create a comfortable and visually appealing environment.

  • Shielding Integration: Coordination with lead or RF shielding systems ensuring regulatory compliance and patient safety..

  • Safety & Compliance Features:Incorporation of radiation signage, emergency power, fire safety measures, and safety zoning.

Supported Imaging Modalities:

  • MRI
  • CT
  • X-Ray
  • PET-CT
  • Fluoroscopy
  • Mammography
