ABOUT ASTRA SHIELD

Astra Shield LLC is a UAE-based engineering company specializing in complete turnkey shielding solutions for high-performance environments. We design, fabricate, install, and test Electromagnetic Interference (EMI), Radio Frequency (RF), Magnetic Shielding, and Radiation Shielding, HEMP Shielding, SEM/TEM Shielding, EMP protection, SCIF facilities, Architectural EMI Shielding and EMI Shielding Paint across a wide range of sectors including defence, medical, industrial, research, and critical infrastructure.

At Astra Shield, we pride ourselves on delivering end-to-end project execution. From initial consultation and site evaluation to engineering design, precision manufacturing, on-site installation, and final certification and testing—we handle every aspect of the shielding process under one roof. This integrated approach ensures high-quality results, reduced project timelines, and strict adherence to international standards.
